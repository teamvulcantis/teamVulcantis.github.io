We believe that there is an amazing amount to be learned by working in real-time environment, alongside like-minded peers.
We named our team vulcan because that is the roman god of machinery. This links it to the project we are working with.

F1 in Schools

An international STEM (science, technology, engineering, mathematics) competition for school children (aged 9-19), in which groups of 3-6 students have to design and manufacture a miniature "car" out of the official F1 Modal Block using CAD/CAM design tools.

Design

Students use Autodesk Software to create and design their Formula1 Racing Cars.

Test

Students use Virtual Reality and Wind tunnels software to analyse cardesigns velocities, pressure, vibration, analysis and more.

Race

After completing their F1 car projects students compete in the following events regionally, nationally and globally.
